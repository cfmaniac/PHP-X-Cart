Where We Ship

If your order does not contain wine, we ship to all states. Cooper’s Hawk is only allowed to ship wine to states where allowed by law. A person of at least 21 years of age must be present to sign for packages that contain alcohol shipped by UPS. Due to Indiana state law we can ship alcohol to your address if you are a Wine Club member only. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact our Member & Guest Services at 888-440-WINE.
